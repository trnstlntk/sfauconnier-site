I was one of the co-founders of a successful ‘peer to peer’ (noncommercial, voluntary) car sharing group in Ghent, Belgium – where I lived and studied from 1991 till approximately 2000. We started with one car (an old VW Golf), and soon a second and third car were brought into the shared pool. After I moved away from Ghent, other people took over and did an amazing job in maintaining and growing the system!
